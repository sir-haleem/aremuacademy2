Hon. Justice L.O Aremu's

Academy For Executive Diploma Studies

Accommodation

For students outside Oyo State arrangement can be made for accommodation at a fee.

In addition, the Academy's activities include a program for young persons who have to work in Law offices. So as to give them the necessary training to enable them to gain the proper skills required of paralegals, and this in turn will make them skilled workers. Students with a minimum of five credits can take the first step into the legal profession and train to be legal executive I.e. a qualified Legal Executive.

We also organize seminars/workshops for personnel of the courts Customary, Magistrates, High Courts Corporate Lawyers, Police, Customs, and Immigration staff and others.

With ILEX in Nigeria the field is open for skilled supporting staff in Law Offices who can manage the Legal support work skillfully. The centre has spacious classrooms, a well stocked Law Library and seasoned Lecturers and renowned Professors.
