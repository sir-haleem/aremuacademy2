The Centre for Legal Studies which has now metamorphosed into Justice Aremu's Academy for Basic Studies was established in 2005 by Hon. Justice & Cheif Mrs. L.O. Aremu. It operates in Oyo State and Lagos State, with the approval of the state Government, and Federal Ministry of Education to run a continuing education centre for enhancement of skills for Judicial Officers, Law graduates, Undergraduates, Court Registrars, Law Clerks, Local Government workers, Bankers and persons seeking admission into University by direct entry. We train Secretaries to specialize as legal secretaries. Another aspect of the activities of the Centre is the Bar vocational course for New Wigs to equip them to be able to establish their own chambers, and how to apply the knowledge acquired at the Law school without the necessity for a period of pupilage. This course is to give the NEW WIGS seven basic skills.

POLICY OBJECTIVES

The Academy provides remedial courses that will correct the problems which conflicting educational policies have perpetrated by lowering the standard of legal education in Nigeria, whereby self expression is now at a discount for many lawyers, the indebth of legal knowledge is a luxury to many legal practitioners especially victims of such policies. Values essential to high legal culture, integrity and decorum are fast fading among Nigerian Lawyers. Legal morals and decorum have been neglected by the policies being created. The Teaching of these have been completely neglected both at the Universities and at the Law School. The Academy is poised to correct all these.
